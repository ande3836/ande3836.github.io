Frictionless Motion Air Track

Mechanical Design & Prototyping

Project Summary

This project involved designing and manufacturing a linear air track to facilitate physics experiments requiring near-zero friction. Using SolidWorks for the initial design and 3D printing for the brackets, the system creates a cushion of air that allows gliders to move smoothly.

Key challenges included ensuring airtight sealing along the plenum and calibrating the airflow for consistent pressure distribution.

Tools Used:
SolidWorks 3D Printing Prototyping
