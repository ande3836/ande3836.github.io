Parkinsons Walking Assist Belt

Assistive Technology & Product Design

Project Summary

This prototype was designed to assist patients with Parkinson's disease in overcoming "freezing of gait." The belt utilizes a visual cueing system—projecting a laser line on the floor in front of the user—to stimulate motor initiation and allow for more fluid walking.

The design focused on comfort, portability, and ease of use, ensuring the device could be worn discreetly while providing the necessary visual stimulus during daily activities.
