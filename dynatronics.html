Dynatronics D9502 Consultation

Medical Device Repair & Analysis

Project Summary

This project involved a private consultation for the Colorado Mesa University Athletic Training Department regarding a malfunctioning Dynatronics D9502 medical device.

The scope of work included disassembling the unit to diagnose internal failures, analyzing the circuit board for damaged components, and providing a technical recommendation on the feasibility of repair versus replacement.
